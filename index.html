Game of life

This is "Game of life". It symulates life, the cells symulates life or death. White means "life", black means "death".
To play, square(cell) has to be filled by click. When you click on a cell it becomes alive. When you fullfill as many cells as you desired, just hit "enter" or click "Let it live!" button to see how life will evolve. After becoming bored just hit "enter" or click "Let it STOP!" button to stop the life. By making different combinations of colored cells, different effect will appear.
